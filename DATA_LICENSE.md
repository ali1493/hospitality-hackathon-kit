## What the data is

Every record in every table in `tracks/*/data/` is synthetic. Guest names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, identifiers, and every other field are generated by [Faker](https://faker.readthedocs.io/) using `seed=42`. No real personally identifiable information from any real guest, employee, or operator appears anywhere in this repository.

The data is modeled on the *shape* of real mid-market hospitality systems: PMS reservation lifecycles (Cloudbeds, Mews, Opera shape), channel-event models (SiteMinder, RateGain), housekeeping conventions (HotSOS, Quore), post-stay engagement (Revinate, Cendyn, TrustYou), online review platforms (Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com), BC Employment Standards Act constraints on shift scheduling, and CASL consent semantics on guest communications. The shape is real. The content is fabricated.
